Detailed Description

This base class represents the connection of an inductive actuator Device to the corresponding SwitchModule.

An inductive actuator may be one of the following Devices:

  • A form signal (FormSignal)
  • A regular switch (RegularSwitch)
  • A double cross switch (DoubleCrossSwitch)
